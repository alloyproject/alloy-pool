Pool's FAQ

I've been mining for X hours and my pending balance has not changed. Why?

The pending balance will increase only when the pool finds a block and the block has matured. A matured block means that 60 additional blocks need to be found. You can track this on the "Pool Blocks" page. This means that it can take 3-5 hours after your FIRST share submission to get your first payment.

My IP is banned / I'm getting message "your IP is banned"

The pool validates each share that miners are sending. If the pool detects that a miner is sending invalid/bad shares in a short period of time it will automatically ban them for 10 minutes

What is the cause of the invalid shares?

Usually too much overclocking or too low of a difficulty. Try a higher port (5555/7777) or FIXEDDIFF to see if that fixes the issue. See: "Getting Started"

What is an orphaned block? Why are blocks red on the "Pool Blocks" page?

A great explanation of the orphaned block concept can be found here: What are Orphaned Blocks

There is no reward for orphaned blocks, it's just an aspect of mining.

I did not receive the payment and have been mining for days! What is going on?

The problem is either your hashrate is too low to make the minimum payment or the problem is using old wallet code. Download the newest version from here: Alloy Downloads

I want to join the pool. How can I start mining?

This pool is anonymous and free for anyone. If you wish to mine with us simply start from "Getting Started" page.

I don't need you or your pool, I'm going to solo mine myself to riches! What do you think about that?

You can solomine this coin using the wallet software, but it will take a LONG time to find a block and you may never find one. Using a pool allows you to get paid on a regular basis.
